Hobbies & Interests

In my spare time I play the bagpipes and have been doing so for nine years. Currently I am playing with the Buchan pipeband in grade 2, 2016 is my first year playing with Buchan pipeband.

Prior to joining Buchan pipebane I played for Deeside Caledonia pipebane in Aberdeen for four years, also in grade 2.
